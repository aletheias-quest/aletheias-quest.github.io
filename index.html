Aletheia (ἀλήθεια) — "unconcealment", the un-forgetting of what was hidden. In Greek myth, the river Lethe steals memory; Aletheia restores it. Our quest is to recover, from a language model's internals and behavior, what it has chosen to conceal.

The Quest

Detection teams build lie detectors with both black-box and white-box access to a suite of LLMs induced to lie in different ways. Datasets are produced in advance by nine independent dataset-creation teams. The competition runs on the National Deep Inference Fabric (NDIF) via NNsight — no local GPU required.
